    ABOUT

    As you leave the powder white sands and calm turquoise waters of Maho Bay, you travel a national park mountain road bordered by towering palm trees and other lush vegetation. At 1000 feet you ascend a steep private drive and behold a gorgeous Caribbean villa opening to spectacular views; you've arrived at Ixora.

    Ixora is the former home of the late Stephen Ambrose bestselling author and historian. The HBO series Band of Brothers was based on his book.

    Your sense of awe will be further enhanced as you step inside Ixora and survey this very beautiful and private, 5 bedroom, 4 bath, luxury home. Ixora consists of 3 traditionally styled buildings connected by decks, tiled walkways, and a garden patio.

    A 60 foot pool, surrounded by a 75 foot deck, a dining gazebo, abundant deck furniture, and stunning views provide a delightful daytime playground. At night the low voltage site lights, the breathtaking view, tranquil privacy and the fabled Caribbean moonlight all combine to produce an irresistibly romantic, peaceful, and relaxing evening setting.

    GUEST REVIEW

    Amazing views, close to the best beach

    This place is to die for. The views are incredible if you are addicted to sunrise/sunsets like me. A mimosa or glass of wine and it melted away any remnant of life stresses.

    The location is very close to my favorite beach, Maho. This beach is gorgeous and has the best sea turtle swimming.

    Me and the girls had a blast. The house is appointed perfectly with rooms on each side to provide separation if needed and a central building for gathering/cooking. The pool is perfect even if you swim for exercise.

    Owners and management were very helpful when I asked a million questions about restaurants and island.

    Stay here... I know I will again.
